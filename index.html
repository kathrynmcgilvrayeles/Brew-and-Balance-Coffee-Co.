Resolving Complaints -30%

Goal: Reduce the time managers spend sorting out customer issues on the floor.

The Blended Learning Approach

A modern approach combining digital tools with human guidance.

1. Core eLearning

Short digital modules covering the psychology of customer service and the 'Brew & Balance standard'.

Benefit: Flexible, self-paced, and ensures staff have basic knowledge.

2. AI 'Angry Customer'

A safe practice environment where staff text or talk with an AI trained to act like an upset cafe customer.

Benefit: Allows staff to practice calming upset customers without real-world risk.

3. Manager Coaching

Focused observation and feedback sessions on the floor, guided by data from the AI practice.

Benefit: Personal connection to apply the learning and build strong relationships.

Why an 'Adaptive' Pilot?

Moving away from one-size-fits-all training to personalised skill development.

Traditional Training

Everyone takes a 60-minute module and a standard quiz. This wastes time for experienced staff and may not fully support new staff.

Adaptive Training

The AI analyses the staff member's responses. If they struggle with empathy, the system alerts the manager to focus on this during coaching.

The Return on Investment (ROI) of the Pilot

Reducing Risk

Testing this new technology on a small group prevents an expensive, flawed rollout across the whole company.

Saving Time

By identifying exact gaps, managers can spend 15 minutes coaching the right skills, instead of 60 minutes covering everything.
